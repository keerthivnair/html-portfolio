The Best Movies According to Keerthi

My top 3 movies of all-time

Yeh Jawani yeh Deewani

This is my favourite bollywoodfilm. It shows the deep bonds of friendship, love and the whole movie as such is so genuinely feel-good .

Devasuram

This is an old classic Lalettan movie( for the malayalis out there who know this pillar of mollywood). I love his acting and the nostalgic feel, the nuances, and the fierceness of the heroine and the reformed rake arc of the hero.

The Karate Kid 1910

No words other than....Jackie Chan and his witty, kickass young protege. This movie was my childhood inspiration. I love it!!
